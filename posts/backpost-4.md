Title: Georgia Tech Invention Studio Sign Date: 2013-01-12

With the expansion of the Invention Studio at Georgia Tech, the shop was in need of a new sign and true to our maker culture a few other students and myself decided to build one!

It is made out of 1/8" aluminum with 3/4" round aluminum stand offs with all of the letters cut out on our Waterjet. It is lit by strips of RGB LEDs that are connected to a controller in the wall with an RF remote.

[Check it out here!](http://thebuildingexperiment.com/Georgia-Tech-Invention-Studio-Sign)
