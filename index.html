About

I'm a mechanical engineer by training. Out of Georgia Tech I went to Quirky and spent my first years building prototypes, turning sketches into things you could pick up and use. That's still the part I love most. Building something real.

From there I moved into infrastructure and reliability, then into leading teams. Edge and platform at Squarespace. Edge-AI and custom hardware at Numina. Data observability at Datadog now. The technology keeps changing. The job underneath it doesn't. I build teams that move fast because they trust each other, and I keep them aimed at problems real customers actually have.

I'm happiest where software meets the physical world. Sensors, hardware, data about something actually happening out there. And I want to be the kind of manager people do their best work for.
